About our company

The amount of by-products of production has increased hundreds of times over the past decade, which required new approaches to solving this issue.

The Tecoil Research and Production Enterprise was established to develop and implement fundamentally new approaches for the development of by-products of oil production (fusions, tank slops, hydrofusions, sapstocks). In our practice, we do not use waste words as these products are raw materials for obtaining high-tech products of export quality. The company includes experienced specialists in the field of chemistry, microbiology, technology, chemists, physicists, microbiologists, experienced technologists and skilled workers.

The company Tecoil fundamentally differs from others in the complexity of approaches and the originality of technical and technological solutions, taking into account the requirements of the market and the economic situation.
