About our company

The amount of by-products has increased hundreds of times over the past decade, which required new approaches to solving this issue.

The Ucoil Research and Production Enterprise was established to develop and implement fundamentally new approaches for the development of by-products of oil production (fusions, tank slops, hydrofusions, sapstocks). In our practice, we do not use word "wastes", because these products are raw materials for obtaining high-tech products of export quality. The company includes experienced specialists in the field of chemistry, microbiology, technology, chemists, physicists, microbiologists, experienced technologists and skilled workers.

Ucoil fundamentally differs from others by complexity of approaches and the originality of technical and technological solutions, also complaining requirements of the market and the economic situation.
